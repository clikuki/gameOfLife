Controls

  • To add/delete a cell, click on the cell.
  • To pause, press the space bar
  • To zoom in and out, use the scroll wheel.
  • To move around, use WASD or the arrow keys.
  • To restart, press the R key.

Note: Zooming is not implemented very well.

Parameters

In order for these to take effect, focus/click on the canvas, then press R to restart.
